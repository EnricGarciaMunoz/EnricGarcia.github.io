Who is
Enric Garcia?

Hi there! I'm Enric Garcia, and I consider myself a passionate person about data projects. I currently live in Barcelona, and since I was doing my university studies, I knew I wanted to have a career in Data Analysis and Data Science, more specifically business-oriented projects, and focusing on communicating with data. That's why I decided to create this portfolio, in which I want to share interesting projects I've done, and that are suitable for different fields.

In terms of experience, I've been working for more than 5 years in different areas related to data, mostly in Data Analysis and Data Science, which has allowed me to have a very complete and global vision of data projects in different companies and contexts. I have been working as Head of Data in my current job for more than 2 years now, leading Data specialists and Data projects of all kinds, from data flows to reporting, including Machine Learning or Predictive Analytics projects.

I feel also fortunate to be able to have another cimplementary job, that is one of my passions: teaching. I currently teach about Data projects and Digital Analytics tools in a Master's degree at La Salle. This allows me to reinforce my ability to communicate with data in an effective and simple way, and to promote the use of Data in the day-to-day working life of my students.

In terms of studies, I studied a degree in Industrial Engineering, and after finishing it, I did a master's degree in E-commerce and another in Data Science (which I am currently finishing), in order to be able to have a vision of the Data Projects linked to the reality of nowadays digital business reality of nowadays digital business.

I sincerely hope you enjoy the content and projects you will find in this portfolio, and you know that if you want to contact me, you can do it without any problem through LinkedIn, or any of the ways I show below.
